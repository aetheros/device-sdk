Publish a message to the MQTT broker on a topic

Parameters
topicthe MQTT topic to publish on
qosThe level of reliability and predictability required. These values correspond directly to MQTT quality of service levels. 0 - Message delivery at most once. 1 - Message delivery at least once. 2 - Message delivery exactly once.
payloaddata payload to send in this message
retainWhether to treat this as a MQTT retained message. MQTT retained messages are stored by the broker and sent to clients who subscribe to this topic at a later time, in addition to clients already subscribed to this topic.
