struct  Packet
 struct struct sent from the Waveform Provider service containing stream and sample information. As the service detects errors, sync will be incremented. sequence will increment normally, but be reset when sync changes. So, under normal successfull operation, consecutive packets will have the same sync value, but increasing sequence values. If sync changes, there was a capture error and samples were lost. If sync does not change, but sequence increased by more than one, then at least one packet was lost between the service and the application. More...
